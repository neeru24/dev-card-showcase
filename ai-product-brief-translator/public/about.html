About This Project

What is AI Product Brief Translator?

This tool helps startup teams, freelancers, and hackathon squads quickly turn product ideas into actionable roadmaps, user stories, design specs, and tasks. It is designed for clarity, speed, and collaboration.

How It Works

  • Enter your product idea or brief in the main form.
  • The tool generates a structured roadmap, user stories, design specs, and tasks.
  • Copy and share sections with your team instantly.

Why Use It?

  • Reduce team misalignment and rework.
  • Accelerate project kickoff and planning.
  • Ensure everyone is on the same page from day one.

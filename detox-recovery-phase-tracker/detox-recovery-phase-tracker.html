üß¨ Detox Phases Explained

Preparation Phase

1-3 days: Gentle dietary changes, reduce processed foods, increase water intake. Common symptoms: Mild fatigue, slight headaches.

Active Detox Phase

3-7 days: Body actively eliminating toxins. Peak symptoms: Headaches, fatigue, skin changes, mood swings.

Recovery Phase

1-2 weeks: Symptoms subside, energy returns. Focus on rebuilding gut health and nutrient replenishment.

Maintenance Phase

Ongoing: Maintain healthy habits. Monitor for any recurring symptoms and adjust lifestyle accordingly.

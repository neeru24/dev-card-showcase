Prevention & Relief Tips

20-20-20 Rule

Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds to reduce eye strain.

Blue Light Filters

Use blue light filters on devices, especially in the evening, to reduce headache triggers.

Hydration

Stay hydrated throughout the day. Dehydration can worsen screen-induced headaches.

Screen Breaks

Take regular breaks from screens. Try the 52-17 rule: 52 minutes work, 17 minutes break.

Display Settings

Adjust brightness and contrast to comfortable levels. Position screens at eye level.

Ergonomics

Maintain proper posture and ensure adequate lighting to reduce strain on eyes and neck.

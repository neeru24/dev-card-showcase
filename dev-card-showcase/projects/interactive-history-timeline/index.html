Key Features:

  • Interactive Timeline: Click different eras to explore historical content
  • Learning Content: Each era displays key facts with typing animation effects
  • Quiz Questions: Test your knowledge with era-specific questions
  • Progress Tracking: Visual progress bars track your learning journey

The feature is designed to be lightweight and educational, focusing on clarity and usability.

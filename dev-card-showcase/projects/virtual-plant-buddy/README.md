# Virtual Plant Buddy

Care for a virtual plant that grows and reacts to your daily check-ins, mood, and productivity. Encourages positive habits and mindfulness.

## Features
- Daily check-in with mood and productivity
- Plant grows and reacts based on your input
- Visual feedback and plant status
- Check-in history
- Modern, friendly UI

## Usage
1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Check in daily, select your mood and productivity.
3. Watch your plant grow and react!
4. View your check-in history below the plant.

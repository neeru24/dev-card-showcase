Breathing Tips

Find a Comfortable Position

Sit or lie down with your spine straight and shoulders relaxed.

Focus on Your Breath

Pay attention to the sensation of air entering and leaving your body.

Be Patient

It's normal for your mind to wander. Gently bring your focus back to your breath.

Practice Regularly

Consistency is key. Even 5 minutes daily can make a difference.

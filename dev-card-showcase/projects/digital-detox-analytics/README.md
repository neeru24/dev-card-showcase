# Digital Detox Analytics

A comprehensive web application for tracking and analyzing digital detox sessions, helping users build healthier relationships with technology through mindful offline activities and progress monitoring.

### Session Logging
- **Activity Types**: Track various offline activities (reading, exercise, meditation, social interaction, hobbies, nature time, sleep)

- **Mood Tracking**: Record mood before and after sessions to measure wellbeing impact

## üöÄ Usage

### Getting Started
1. **Open the Application**: Navigate to the Digital Detox Analytics
2. **Set Goals**: Define your daily detox time and streak goals
3. **Log Your First Session**: Record your offline activities
4. **Track Progress**: Monitor your digital wellness journey
5. **Review Insights**: Use recommendations to optimize your detox practice

### Logging Sessions
1. **Select Activity**: Choose from predefined offline activities or select "Other"
2. **Enter Duration**: Record how long you spent in the detox activity (in minutes)
3. **Device Restrictions**: Check which devices you avoided during the session
4. **Mood Assessment**: Rate your mood before and after the session
5. **Add Notes**: Include any reflections or observations
6. **Submit**: Log the session and update your analytics

### Setting Goals
- **Daily Detox Time**: Set a target for minutes of daily offline activity
- **Streak Goals**: Define how many consecutive days you want to maintain detox habits
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your advancement toward goals with visual indicators

### Understanding Analytics
- **Today's Progress**: Current day detox time and goal achievement
- **Weekly Average**: Average daily detox time over the past week
- **Current Streak**: Consecutive days with detox activity
- **Mood Improvement**: Average mood change from detox sessions

## üéØ Digital Wellness Tips

### Building Better Habits
1. **Start Small**: Begin with 15-30 minute sessions to build confidence
2. **Create Triggers**: Associate detox time with existing routines (after meals, before bed)
3. **Designate Spaces**: Create phone-free zones in your home
4. **Use Technology Wisely**: Set screen time limits and app restrictions
5. **Find Accountability**: Share your goals with friends or family

### Common Challenges & Solutions
- **FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out)**: Remember that most digital content isn't urgent
- **Habit Loops**: Replace phone-checking with a healthy alternative activity
- **Social Pressure**: Politely explain your digital boundaries to others
- **Work Requirements**: Set clear work/non-work technology boundaries

### Advanced Strategies
- **Digital Sabbaths**: Designate entire days for complete disconnection
- **Technology Fasting**: Multi-day detox periods for deeper reset
- **Mindful Technology Use**: Be intentional about when and how you use devices
- **Digital Minimalism**: Regularly evaluate and remove unnecessary digital tools

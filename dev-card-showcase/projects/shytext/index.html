Peripheral

I am not a fan of being watched.

When you look directly at me, I lose my focus.

Shadows of my former self remain, yet the clarity vanishes.

If you wish to read my secrets, you must look elsewhere.

The corners of your eye are where I feel most safe.

Try to catch me if you can, but I will only blur away.

Attention is a weight I cannot carry.

In the margins of your vision, I finally find my voice.

I am the whisper you hear when you aren't listening.

Keep your distance, and I shall remain clear.

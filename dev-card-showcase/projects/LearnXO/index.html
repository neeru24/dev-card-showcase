Project Description

LearnXO is a self-learning Tic-Tac-Toe AI that improves through experience by remembering past game outcomes. Unlike traditional implementations, it does not rely on predefined strategies. Instead, it trains itself over multiple rounds, adapting its predictions and gameplay based on previous results.

Difficulty Levels Overview

  • Level 1 — Learner: Easy, experimental, and forgiving
  • Level 2 — Adaptive: Competitive, responsive, and strategic
  • Level 3 — Beyond the Board: Narrative-driven, no draws, rule-breaking victory

Project Scope

What the project includes:

  • Single-player experience
  • Adaptive AI behavior
  • No multiplayer
  • No external libraries

What is out of scope:

  • No human vs human mode
  • No real ML training
  • No backend services

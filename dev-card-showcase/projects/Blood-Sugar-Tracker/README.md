A comprehensive web-based blood glucose monitoring interface designed to help diabetic users track their daily glucose fluctuations easily and effectively.

## Features

- **Glucose Logging**: Input blood sugar levels with date, time, and optional notes
- **Daily Trend Graphs**: Visualize glucose trends over time using interactive charts
- **Personalized Diet Advice**: Receive health recommendations based on your glucose readings
- **Local Data Storage**: All data is stored locally in your browser for privacy
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices

## How to Use

1. **Log Your Glucose**: Enter the date, time, glucose level (in mg/dL), and any notes
2. **View Trends**: Check the daily trend chart to see your glucose patterns
3. **Get Advice**: Read personalized diet and health recommendations based on your latest reading
4. **Track History**: View your recent entries in the list below

## Health Guidelines

The app provides advice based on standard blood glucose ranges:
- **Low (< 70 mg/dL)**: Immediate action needed - consume fast-acting carbohydrates
- **Normal (70-140 mg/dL)**: Good control - maintain current habits
- **Elevated (140-180 mg/dL)**: Monitor closely - consider lifestyle adjustments
- **High (> 180 mg/dL)**: Consult healthcare provider - may need medical intervention

## Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript (ES6+)
- Chart.js for data visualization
- Local Storage API for data persistence

## Privacy & Data

All glucose data is stored locally in your browser's local storage. No data is transmitted to external servers, ensuring your health information remains private and secure.

# Mood Reflection Aquarium

Log your daily mood and watch your virtual aquarium change! Happy days add colorful fish, neutral days add plants, and sad days add clouds or algae. The aquarium becomes a visual diary of your emotional trends.

## Features
- Log your mood each day
- Aquarium visualizes mood history
- Happy: adds fish, Neutral: adds plants, Sad: adds clouds/algae
- Mood history list
- Modern, beautiful UI

## Usage
1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Log your mood each day.
3. Watch your aquarium change and grow!

About HoloClass Builder

What is HoloClass Builder?

Teachers create interactive 3D micro-lessons (science, history, engineering) in browser AR/3D scenes. Drag and drop objects to build lessons and share with a link.

How It Works

  • Choose a template lesson scene.
  • Drag and drop objects to build your micro-lesson.
  • Get a shareable class link for students or colleagues.

Why Use It?

  • Make lessons interactive and visual.
  • Perfect for educators, edtech startups, and online tutors.
  • Easy to share and reuse micro-lessons.

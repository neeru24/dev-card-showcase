About Dream Heist Simulator

What is Dream Heist Simulator?

A browser-based multiplayer strategy game where AI generates unique mission worlds every round. Players choose roles and solve coordinated challenges in real time for dynamic, collaborative experiences.

How It Works

  • Form a 4-player lobby and assign roles.
  • Start a mission on one of two maps.
  • Coordinate to solve challenges and reach branching endings.

Why Use It?

  • Enjoy dynamic, non-repetitive multiplayer gameplay.
  • Build teamwork and strategy skills.
  • Perfect for gamers, friend groups, and team-building.

Oxidative stress occurs when free radicals overwhelm the body's antioxidant defenses. This can damage cells, proteins, and DNA, contributing to aging and various diseases.

Antioxidants neutralize free radicals and protect cellular components. Sources include vitamins C/E, glutathione, superoxide dismutase, and dietary antioxidants from fruits and vegetables.

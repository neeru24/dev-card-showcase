About Second-Brain Autopilot

What is Second-Brain Autopilot?

Connect your notes, bookmarks, docs, and chats. AI auto-builds a structured “knowledge map” and delivers weekly insights, so your personal knowledge never gets lost.

How It Works

  • Link your notes, bookmarks, docs, and chats.
  • AI organizes them into a visual knowledge map.
  • Receive weekly insights and summaries.

Who Uses It?

  • Researchers
  • Creators
  • Founders
  • Students

Running Tips

Optimal Stride Length

Ideal stride length is typically 2.5-3 times your foot length. Focus on quick, light steps rather than long strides.

Cadence Training

Aim for 180 steps per minute. Use a metronome app to maintain consistent rhythm and improve efficiency.

Progressive Overload

Gradually increase your stride length by 1-2cm per week. Combine with strength training for sustainable improvements.

Monitor Fatigue

Track how stride length changes with fatigue. A decrease of 5-10% may indicate overtraining or need for recovery.

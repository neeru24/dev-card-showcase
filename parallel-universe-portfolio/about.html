About Parallel-Universe Portfolio

What is Parallel-Universe Portfolio?

A portfolio site where each “universe” shows a different version of your career path (Developer You, Founder You, Designer You), each with projects, skills, and timeline outcomes. It helps you showcase your potential and versatility.

How It Works

  • Select a universe to view your alternate career path.
  • Each universe displays unique projects, skills, and timeline outcomes.
  • Switch between universes to show your versatility.

Why Use It?

  • Show your potential, not just your history.
  • Stand out as a multi-skilled creator or job switcher.
  • Perfect for students, freelancers, and career explorers.

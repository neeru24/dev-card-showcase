What is Differential Privacy?

Differential privacy is a mathematical framework that provides strong privacy guarantees for individual data points in a dataset. It ensures that the output of a query doesn't reveal whether any particular individual's data was included or not.

Epsilon (Îµ): The privacy parameter. Smaller Îµ means stronger privacy but less accurate results.

Sensitivity: The maximum change in query output when one individual's data changes.

Heavy Metals Overview

Lead (Pb)

Sources: Paint, batteries, contaminated water, industrial emissions

Symptoms: Fatigue, irritability, memory loss, anemia

Mercury (Hg)

Sources: Fish, dental fillings, thermometers, industrial waste

Symptoms: Tremors, memory problems, insomnia, anxiety

Arsenic (As)

Sources: Contaminated water, pesticides, industrial processes

Symptoms: Skin changes, nausea, diarrhea, numbness

Cadmium (Cd)

Sources: Cigarette smoke, batteries, contaminated food

Symptoms: Kidney damage, bone pain, respiratory issues

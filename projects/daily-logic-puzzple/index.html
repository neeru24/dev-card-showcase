Today's Puzzle

Five friends - Alex, Bailey, Casey, Dakota, and Elliot - each have a different favorite dessert: cheesecake, ice cream, pie, tiramisu, and pudding. Use the clues below to determine which dessert each person prefers.

1 Alex doesn't like cheesecake or ice cream.
2 Bailey's favorite is either tiramisu or pudding.
3 Casey prefers a dessert that starts with the same letter as their name.
4 Dakota's favorite is not ice cream or pie.
5 Elliot and Bailey both dislike cheesecake.
6 The person who likes ice cream is not Dakota or Alex.

Solution & Explanation

Think through each clue systematically to solve the puzzle. Click the button below to reveal the step-by-step solution.

1 From clue #3, Casey prefers cheesecake (both start with 'C').
2 From clue #5, Elliot and Bailey both dislike cheesecake, so they can't have cheesecake (which Casey has).
3 From clue #1, Alex doesn't like cheesecake or ice cream. So Alex can't have cheesecake (Casey has it) or ice cream.
4 From clue #2, Bailey's favorite is either tiramisu or pudding.
5 From clue #4, Dakota's favorite is not ice cream or pie.
6 From clue #6, the person who likes ice cream is not Dakota or Alex. So ice cream must be for either Bailey or Elliot.
7 Since Bailey can only have tiramisu or pudding (clue #2), Elliot must have ice cream.
8 With Elliot having ice cream, Alex can't have ice cream (clue #1), and Dakota can't have ice cream (clue #4). Alex also can't have cheesecake, so Alex must have either pie, tiramisu, or pudding.
9 Dakota can't have ice cream or pie (clue #4), and cheesecake is taken, so Dakota must have either tiramisu or pudding.
10 If both Bailey and Dakota can only have tiramisu or pudding, and there are only one of each dessert, then Alex must have pie.
11 Between Bailey and Dakota, who has tiramisu and who has pudding? Clue #2 says Bailey has either tiramisu or pudding, but doesn't specify which. Since we know all other desserts are assigned, Bailey could have either. Looking for additional constraints: All clues are satisfied regardless of whether Bailey has tiramisu or pudding, and Dakota gets the other.
12 Final solution: Casey = Cheesecake, Elliot = Ice Cream, Alex = Pie, Bailey = Tiramisu or Pudding, Dakota = Pudding or Tiramisu (the opposite of Bailey).
Complete Answer:
Alex: Pie
Bailey: Tiramisu (or Pudding)
Casey: Cheesecake
Dakota: Pudding (or Tiramisu)
Elliot: Ice Cream

The Botanist's Guide

üå± What is this project?

This is a time-series virtual gardening and farming simulation engine. It allows you to build and cultivate your very own digital garden from scratch by planting a variety of specific trees and beautiful flowers.

‚ú® What does it do?

The game provides an interactive plot of land, an inventory of seeds, and a set of gardening tools. Every plot acts as a time machine. As you plant seeds, care for them, and move time forward, the game calculates their exact growth stages to create a living ecosystem.

üéÆ How to Play (Step-by-Step)

  • Step 1: Choose Your Seeds - Look at the Inventory panel on the right. Choose between Tree Seeds (Apple, Orange, Banana) or Flower Seeds (Rose, Sunflower, Lily).
  • Step 2: Plant the Seed - Once a seed is selected, click on any empty square in the left garden area to plant it.
  • Step 3: Care for Plants - Look at the Tools panel on the far right. Select Water (acts as a growth multiplier) or Fertilizer (gives an instant growth boost), and click your planted seeds to apply them.
  • Step 4: Time Travel! - Drag the Time Machine Slider forward to simulate months passing. Watch your plants visually evolve from seeds to fully mature plants based on your care!
  • Step 5: Uproot - Made a mistake? Select the Uproot tool and click a plant to clear the soil.

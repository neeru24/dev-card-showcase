Brewing Guides

Ideal Coffee-to-Water Ratios

Pour Over (V60, Chemex)
1:15 to 1:17 ratio. 30-45 second bloom, total brew time 2-4 minutes.
French Press
1:12 to 1:15 ratio. Steep for 4 minutes, press slowly, serve immediately.
AeroPress
1:10 to 1:16 ratio. Brew time 1-2 minutes, inverted or standard method.
Espresso
1:1.5 to 1:3 ratio. 18-22g coffee, 25-35 second extraction time.
Cold Brew
1:4 to 1:8 ratio for concentrate. Steep 12-24 hours, dilute before serving.

Pro Tips

Grind Size Matters
Use coarse grind for French Press, medium for Pour Over, fine for Espresso.
Water Quality
Use filtered water for better extraction and to avoid off-flavors.
Freshness
Use coffee within 2-4 weeks of roasting for optimal flavor.
Bloom Phase
For pour overs, bloom with 2x coffee weight in water for 30-45 seconds to release CO2.

Strength Guide

1:10 to 1:13 Ratio
Strong, bold coffee. Common for AeroPress or Moka Pot.
1:14 to 1:17 Ratio
Balanced, standard strength. Ideal for most filter methods.
1:18 to 1:20 Ratio
Lighter, more delicate coffee. Can highlight subtle flavors.

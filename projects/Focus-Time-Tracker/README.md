
# Focus Time Tracker üïØÔ∏è

An advanced focus and productivity tracking UI designed to help users build awareness of their deep work time. This tool combines traditional Pomodoro-style timers with productivity analytics and motivational tips.

## Features

### ‚è±Ô∏è Focus Timer
- Customizable focus and break durations
- Start, pause, and reset controls
- Automatic mode switching between focus and break
- Session counter for completed focus periods

### üìä Productivity Stats
- Daily focus time tracking
- Weekly productivity overview
- Streak counter for consecutive days
- Visual chart representation of weekly progress

### üí° Productivity Tips
- Random motivational tips for better focus
- Tips on time management and productivity techniques
- Encourages healthy work habits

## How to Use

1. **Set Timer Durations**: Adjust the focus and break time inputs to your preferred intervals (default: 25 min focus, 5 min break)

2. **Start Focusing**: Click the "Start" button to begin your focus session

3. **Track Progress**: Monitor your daily and weekly statistics in the stats section

4. **Get Inspired**: Click "New Tip" to receive productivity advice

## Technical Details

- **Frontend**: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+)
- **Storage**: Browser localStorage for persistent data
- **Charts**: Custom Canvas-based bar chart
- **Responsive**: Works on desktop and mobile devices

## Browser Support

- Chrome 60+
- Firefox 55+
- Safari 12+
- Edge 79+

## Data Privacy

All data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. No personal information is collected or transmitted.

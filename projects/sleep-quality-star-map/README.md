# Sleep Quality Star Map

Log your sleep quality each night. Good sleep adds bright stars and constellations to a virtual sky, while poor sleep adds clouds or dims the stars. The map visualizes sleep trends over time.

## Features
- Log your sleep quality each night
- Good sleep: bright stars and constellations
- Average: dimmer stars
- Poor: clouds or very dim stars
- Sleep history list
- Modern, beautiful UI

## Usage
1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Log your sleep quality each night.
3. Watch your star map change and grow!

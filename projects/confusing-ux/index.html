Mistakes Made
  • Moving/Trembling Elements
    Elements that move or shake make it difficult for users to click or read content, causing frustration and errors.
  • Poor Color Contrast
    Low contrast between text and background makes content hard to read, especially for users with visual impairments.
  • Overuse of Animations
    Excessive or distracting animations can disorient users and make the interface feel chaotic and unprofessional.
  • Unclear Labels & Instructions
    Vague, confusing, or ironic labels leave users unsure about what to do or what information is required.
  • Inconsistent Styling
    Using multiple fonts, colors, and styles creates visual chaos and makes the interface feel disjointed.
  • Hidden or Moving Buttons
    Buttons that move or are hard to find prevent users from completing tasks and increase cognitive load.
This demo intentionally violates UX best practices to educate designers about what to avoid.
Remember: Good UX should be intuitive, accessible, and user-centered.

About Dream Pattern Analyzer

What is this?

This app helps you log your dreams, analyze them for recurring themes, emotions, and symbols, and visualize patterns over time. It is designed for personal insight and self-reflection.

How does it work?

  • Dreams are stored securely in your browser (localStorage).
  • Text analysis finds common words, emotions, and symbols.
  • Visualizations help you spot patterns and trends.

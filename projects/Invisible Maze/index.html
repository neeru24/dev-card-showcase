How to Play

  • The maze is visible briefly at the start (memorize it!).
  • After preview, walls disappear - navigate from memory.
  • Use Arrow keys or WASD to move.
  • Hit walls to lose lives or get reset (depends on mode).
  • Use Power-ups strategically to help navigate.
  • Use Hints to see walls briefly (limited uses).
  • Complete levels to progress and unlock achievements.
  • Reach the yellow exit to win!

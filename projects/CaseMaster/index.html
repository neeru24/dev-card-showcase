Why Text Transformation?

Confused by all the "Cases"? Here is a cheat sheet for clean writing and coding:

For Developers
CamelCase: Start small, bump in the middle. The standard for JavaScript.
Snake_Case: Words linked by underscores. Python's favorite.
Kebab-Case: Words linked by dashes. Used in URLs and CSS.
For Writers
Title Case: Use this for Headlines, Book Titles, and Headers.
Sentence case: Standard grammar for body text and emails.
Data Cleaning
Trim Lines: Deleting those annoying invisible spaces at the start/end of lines.
Regex: Using patterns to find/replace complex text in bulk.

# Doctor Visit History üßë‚Äç‚öïÔ∏è

A comprehensive web-based medical visit history tracker designed to help users maintain detailed records of their healthcare interactions and improve medical record keeping.

## Features

- **Visit Logging**: Record doctor visits with date, physician details, specialty, diagnosis, medications, and notes
- **Timeline View**: Chronological display of all medical visits with clear visual organization
- **General Notes**: Dedicated section for ongoing health observations, reminders, and important information
- **Local Data Storage**: All medical data stored locally in your browser for complete privacy
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices

## How to Use

1. **Log a Visit**: Fill out the visit form with all relevant medical information
2. **View Timeline**: See all your visits in reverse chronological order (newest first)
3. **Add General Notes**: Use the notes section for ongoing health information or reminders
4. **Track History**: Easily reference past diagnoses, medications, and doctor recommendations

## Data Privacy

All medical information is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. No data is transmitted to external servers, ensuring your health records remain completely private and secure.

## Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript (ES6+)
- Local Storage API for data persistence

## Contributing

This is part of the Dev Card Showcase project. Contributions are welcome! Please follow the project's contribution guidelines.

## Medical Disclaimer

This application is for personal record-keeping purposes only and should not replace official medical records or professional healthcare advice. Always consult with healthcare providers for medical decisions and maintain official medical documentation.

How to Create Your Comic Strip

Step 1: Add Panels

Click "Add Panel" to create comic panels. Drag them around the canvas to arrange your story.

Step 2: Choose Characters

Select characters from the character library to add to your panels.

Step 3: Add Dialogue

Click on dialogue bubbles to add text. Choose from different dialogue styles.

Step 4: Set Backgrounds

Choose background scenes for each panel to set the location of your story.

Step 5: Generate & Export

Click "Generate Comic Strip" to create your final comic, then export or print it!

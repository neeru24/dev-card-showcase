Understanding Stress Capacity

Acute Stress Response

The immediate physiological response to a stressor, involving the sympathetic nervous system and HPA axis activation.

Adaptive Capacity

Your body's ability to respond appropriately to stress and return to homeostasis efficiently.

Allostatic Load

The cumulative wear and tear on the body from chronic stress exposure and inadequate recovery.

Resilience Building

Activities and practices that strengthen your stress response system and improve recovery capacity.

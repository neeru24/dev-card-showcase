Traffic Signals Explained

Understanding Traffic Lights

Traffic signals control the flow of vehicles and pedestrians at intersections. Each color has a specific meaning:

  • Red Light: Stop completely before the stop line or crosswalk.
  • Yellow Light: Prepare to stop. If you're too close to stop safely, proceed with caution.
  • Green Light: Proceed if the intersection is clear and it's safe to do so.

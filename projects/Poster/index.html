Posture Tips

Sit Up Straight

Keep your back straight, shoulders back, and buttocks touching the back of your chair.

Monitor at Eye Level

Position your monitor so the top of the screen is at or slightly below eye level.

Keep Feet Flat

Place your feet flat on the floor or on a footrest, with knees at or below hip level.

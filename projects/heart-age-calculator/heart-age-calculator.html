Understanding Heart Age

What is Heart Age?

Your heart age is an estimate of how old your heart and blood vessels are based on your cardiovascular risk factors. It may be younger or older than your actual age.

Key Risk Factors

  • Blood Pressure: High BP damages artery walls
  • Cholesterol: LDL builds up in arteries, HDL protects
  • Smoking: Damages blood vessels and reduces oxygen
  • Diabetes: Increases cardiovascular risk significantly
  • Physical Activity: Regular exercise strengthens the heart

Improving Your Heart Age

  • Maintain healthy blood pressure (<120/80 mmHg)
  • Achieve optimal cholesterol levels
  • Quit smoking and avoid secondhand smoke
  • Control blood sugar if diabetic
  • Exercise regularly (150 minutes/week moderate activity)
  • Eat a heart-healthy diet (fruits, vegetables, whole grains)
  • Maintain healthy weight
  • Manage stress and get adequate sleep

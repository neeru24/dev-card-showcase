A comprehensive mental health monitoring application designed to help users track their stress levels, identify patterns, and access coping strategies for better mental wellness.

## Features

- **Stress Scale Tracking**: Rate your stress on a 1-10 scale with visual feedback
- **Mood Monitoring**: Track emotional states alongside stress levels
- **Trigger Identification**: Log what causes stress for better awareness
- **Trend Analysis**: Interactive charts showing stress patterns over time
- **Mood Distribution**: Visual breakdown of emotional states
- **Personalized Coping Tips**: Dynamic strategies based on current stress levels
- **AI Insights**: Intelligent analysis of stress trends and recommendations
- **Local Data Storage**: Complete privacy with browser-based storage

## How to Use

### Daily Stress Check
1. **Rate Your Stress**: Use the slider to indicate your current stress level (1-10)
2. **Select Mood**: Choose your current emotional state
3. **Note Triggers**: Record what might be causing your stress
4. **Add Notes**: Include any additional thoughts or observations
5. **Log Entry**: Save your stress check for tracking

### View Analytics
- **Current Status**: See your latest stress level with color-coded indicators
- **Trend Charts**: Analyze stress patterns over 7 days, 30 days, or 3 months
- **Mood Patterns**: Understand your emotional distribution
- **Insights**: Get AI-powered analysis and recommendations

### Coping Strategies
- **Dynamic Tips**: Receive personalized coping strategies based on your stress level
- **Low Stress**: Mindfulness and social connection techniques
- **Moderate Stress**: Breathing exercises and physical activity
- **High Stress**: Immediate relief techniques and professional help suggestions

## Stress Level Guide

### 1-3: Low Stress
- **Characteristics**: Feeling calm, in control, relaxed
- **Management**: Maintain current healthy habits
- **Focus**: Preventive wellness and self-care

### 4-7: Moderate Stress
- **Characteristics**: Some tension, manageable challenges
- **Management**: Active coping strategies and lifestyle adjustments
- **Focus**: Stress reduction techniques and work-life balance

### 8-10: High Stress
- **Characteristics**: Feeling overwhelmed, difficulty coping
- **Management**: Immediate intervention and professional support
- **Focus**: Crisis management and comprehensive stress relief

## Important Disclaimers

### Not a Substitute for Professional Help
- This tool is for personal monitoring and awareness only
- Not a replacement for professional mental health treatment
- Always consult qualified mental health professionals for serious concerns

### Data Limitations
- Self-reported data may not be clinically accurate
- Browser storage has limitations and can be cleared
- Regular backups of important mental health data recommended

### Crisis Support
- If experiencing severe stress, anxiety, or suicidal thoughts, contact:
  - Emergency services (911 in US)
  - Crisis hotlines (988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline in US)
  - Mental health professionals
  - Trusted friends or family

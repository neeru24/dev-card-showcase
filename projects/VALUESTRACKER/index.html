How to use this tracker:

  1. Drag values from the "All Values" panel to the "Your Ranked Values" panel
  2. Reorder your ranked values by dragging them within the right panel
  3. Your top value will be shown at the top of the ranked list
  4. Click "Save Ranking" to save your values to your browser's local storage
  5. Click "Reset" to clear your ranking and start over

Your values are saved locally in your browser. They will remain until you clear your browser data.

TrafficGod Simulation

Welcome to TrafficGod, a microscopic traffic simulation powered by the Intelligent Driver Model (IDM).

How it Works

  • Road: A circular track is used to simulate infinite traffic flow.
  • Physics: Each vehicle calculates its acceleration based on the gap to the leader and current speed (IDM).
  • Emergence: Phantom traffic jams appear spontaneously when density is high or drivers are aggressive.

Controls

  • Density: Number of vehicles on the road.
  • Acceleration (a): How quickly cars speed up.
  • Politeness (T): Safe following time distance. Lower is more aggressive.
  • Chaos: Random braking probability.

Built with Vanilla JS, HTML5 Canvas, and Math.
